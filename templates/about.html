Who We Are

Polytechnic English School was established in 2005 with a vision to provide holistic education that nurtures academic excellence, character development, and leadership skills in students. Located in the heart of the city, we offer a vibrant and safe environment where students are empowered to discover their potential and grow into responsible global citizens.

Our Mission

Our mission is to inspire and equip students with the knowledge, values, and skills necessary to thrive in an ever-changing world. We believe education is not just about learning facts, but cultivating curiosity, creativity, and compassion.

Principal’s Message

“At Polytechnic English School, we aim to build a nurturing environment where every child is valued, challenged, and celebrated. We focus on holistic education that fosters academic excellence, ethical leadership, and social responsibility. We invite parents and the community to partner with us in this exciting journey of growth and discovery.”

- Mr. Khemraj prasad, Principal
