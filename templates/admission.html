Admission Process

  1. Fill out the application form (online or offline).
  2. Submit required documents (birth certificate, previous school report, etc.).
  3. Attend an interaction/interview session (for certain grades).
  4. Receive admission confirmation and pay the initial fee.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Nursery: Minimum age 3 years as of March 31, 2025
  • KG to Grade 10: Based on prior class completion and availability

Fee Structure (2025-26)

  • Nursery to KG: ₹25,000 per term
  • Classes 1 to 5: ₹30,000 per term
  • Classes 6 to 10: ₹35,000 per term

*Transportation and optional activities charged separately

Important Dates

  • Application Opens: 1st July 2025
  • Last Date to Apply: 20th August 2025
  • Entrance Tests/Interviews: 1st to 5th September 2025
