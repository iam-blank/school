Academic Programs

Our academic structure is designed to build a strong foundation and gradually nurture intellectual, emotional, and social development.

  • Pre-Primary (Nursery & KG): Focus on play-based learning, motor skills, and language development.
  • Primary School (Grades 1–5): Emphasis on reading, writing, numeracy, and conceptual learning.
  • Middle School (Grades 6–8): Introduction to critical thinking, science labs, and project-based learning.
  • Secondary School (Grades 9–10): In-depth subject knowledge and board exam preparation.

Curriculum Overview

We follow the CBSE curriculum aligned with NEP 2020. Our syllabus balances academic rigor with creativity and innovation.

  • Core subjects: English, Hindi, Mathematics, Science, Social Science
  • Languages: Sanskrit/French (Grade 5 onwards)
  • Skill-based subjects: Computer Science, General Knowledge, Art & Craft

Learning Facilities

  • Smart Classrooms with digital content
  • Science & Computer Laboratories
  • Well-stocked Library
  • Math Lab & Language Lab
  • Interactive AV Rooms

Assessment & Evaluation

We follow Continuous and Comprehensive Evaluation (CCE) to assess students through class tests, projects, presentations, and term-end exams.

  • Monthly tests and assignments
  • Mid-term and Final term exams
  • Report cards with academic and co-scholastic feedback

Co-curricular & Enrichment

We believe learning goes beyond textbooks. Students are encouraged to participate in:

  • Music, Dance, Drama, Art
  • Sports: Football, Cricket, Yoga, Athletics
  • Clubs: Eco Club, Debate, Coding Club, Scouts & Guides
  • Annual Science & Cultural Fest
